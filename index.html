Naruto Series Character's Abiliy

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto is able to use the Wind Release: Rasengan after he molds his chakra nature into the original Rasengan form. Naruto's chakra affinity is Air, which gives his ninjutsu a precise cutting edge. By combining it with his Rasengan, he's able to create the Wind Release: Rasengan.

Madara Uchiha

As an Uchiha, Madara is able to use Fire style jutsu. Madara's fire ball jutsu can encompass a large distances and can swallow many opponents. Madara can also use the Wood style jutsu since he implanted Hashirama's cell into his body.

Jiraiya

Jiraiya could utilise the natures of Fire, Earth, Water, Wind, Yin and Yang Release. With Fire Release, he could a shoot a blast of fire that he could combine with his toad's oil to make for an even more devastating attack. With Earth Release, he could create a swamp to trap targets in and massive defensive walls.

Minato Namikaze

JUnlike most shinobi, Minato excelled in multiple arts. While he had only 3 nature transformations, he excelled at high-speed combat, teleportation ninjutsu, barrier ninjutsu, taijutsu, sealing jutsu, other space-time ninjutsu, and even some forbidden ninjutsu.

Kakashi Hatake

Since the eye got transplanted into him, Kakashi's ability to copy nearly any technique used against him gave him the moniker Copy Ninja Kakashi ( Kopī Ninja no Kakashi) while utilising many of the Sharingan abilities including genjutsu, seeing through deception.

Boruto Uzumaki

As an Uzumaki, Boruto is shown to have very large reserves of stamina and chakra, able to produce four shadow clones in battle before being properly trained in the Academy. Even after having most of his chakra absorbed, he still retained enough strength to fight effectively and create multiple clones.

Obito Uchiha

Simply put, the power of Obito's Mangekyo Sharingan is known as Kamui. With this ability, Obito gains access to a whole new dimension where he can transport things to, including himself. The ability that he possesses in his left eye and right eye differ greatly.

Shikamaru Nara

Shikamaru's abilities are based on the Shadow Imitation Technique (影真似の術, Kagemane no Jutsu, English TV: "Shadow Possession Jutsu"), the signature technique of his clan, with which he merges his shadow with an opponent's shadow, making them immobilized and forced to mimic Shikamaru's movements.
